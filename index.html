COLLABORATE

We are a group of 30 passionate students serious about designing, building, and sending a satellite into space on a never-before-completed mission. WatSat consists of students with technical expertise across Systems Design, Mechatronics, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, & beyond.

DESIGN

The WatSat team is compromised of several subsystem teams ranging from payload, propulsion, business, structures, data handling, control systems, communication, and power systems. Together the committees work to plan out the design of the satellite in weekly meetings.

DEVELOP

Every two years WatSat, an affiliate of the Waterloo Space Society, enters the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge (CSDC). This year, the purpose of the team is to create a triple cube satellite from off-the-shelf components with the purpose of monitoring changes in the arctic sea-ice.
